About The Conference

Conference Begins In

The Edinburgh Finance Conference (EFC) is one of the largest student-run, non-profit finance conferences in the UK. Our mission is to inspire the leaders of tomorrow through captivating presentations while giving them a chance to connect.

In previous iterations, we have welcomed a broad range of inspiring speakers ranging from managing directors of Goldman Sachs to Fintech leaders such as the CEO of Atom Bank. This year, we want to build upon our previous success and would like to transform EFC into a think tank for all the industry professionals to discuss their current research.

Why EFC?

Our Theme

This year’s theme is ‘Limited Resources, Unlimited Potential’ which is highly relevant to the current geo-economical atmosphere.

Hear from Experts

Bringing leading academics from all disciplines and various universities across the UK together with industry experts, EFC is offering itself as a platform for synergy and problem-solving. We see cross-disciplinary work as a vital driver of pioneering developments.

Networking

We hope that this conference will serve as a platform to encourage dialogue in order to realise this potential.
